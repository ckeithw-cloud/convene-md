# convene.md — Instagram growth plan

**Account:** @convenemd · **Link in bio:** https://convene.md
**Audience:** clinicians with employer-funded CME budgets. The hook in every post: *your hospital pays — pick somewhere worth going.*

## Cadence

3 posts/week to start (Mon / Wed / Sat), scale to daily once a 4-week backlog exists.
Rotate three formats:
1. **Destination spotlight** — aspirational travel photo + one conference (the workhorse)
2. **"This month in surgery"** — carousel of 4–6 upcoming meetings from the list view
3. **Map screenshot** — a region's pins ("11 surgical meetings in Europe this fall")

## Target accounts to engage (follow, comment substantively — no bots)

Handles must be verified in-app before engaging; org names are from our own dataset.
- **Societies (every `organizer` in conferences.js is a lead):** American College of Surgeons, SAGES, ASMBS, AAOS, EACTS, EAES, IFSO, Wilderness Medical Society, Surfers Medical Association, World Extreme Medicine, RACS, RCSI…
- **CME/education:** Mayo Clinic School of CPD, Healio, ACCME-adjacent CME providers, board-prep accounts
- **Physician creators:** surgery residents/attendings who post conference recaps and #MedTwitter/#MedEd crossover creators; prioritize anyone posting from a conference badge
- **Medical-travel adjacent:** locum agencies, physician-finance accounts (they share the "use your CME stipend" audience)

Daily 15-minute loop: comment on 5 posts from target accounts, reply to every comment on ours, follow 10–15 relevant accounts. Manual — automated following gets accounts flagged.

## Monetization: flights first

Hotels usually route through conference discount blocks, so hotel affiliates underperform here. Plan:
1. Owner creates a free **Travelpayouts** account (aggregates Skyscanner/WayAway/Kiwi flight affiliates) → gets a marker ID.
2. We add a **"Find flights →"** button to every popup/list row, deep-linking to a flight search with the conference city + dates prefilled and the affiliate marker attached.
3. Revisit further monetization (sponsored pins, newsletter) once traffic is measurable in Cloudflare Web Analytics.
