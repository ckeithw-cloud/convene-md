# Society-reshare outreach — the highest-leverage growth loop

**The idea:** every convene.md post features a real organization's conference. That org has
every reason to reshare free promotion of their event — and their followers are precisely our
audience (clinicians who attend that kind of meeting). One reshare from a society beats a week
of hashtags.

## The routine (do this with EVERY post)

1. **Tag the org in the post itself** (in the caption and/or on the image). Find their IG handle
   first — search their name in IG.
2. **The day you post, DM them** the short message below. Small/niche societies almost always
   reshare; big ones sometimes do.
3. If they have no IG, tag their **conference** account or the **host venue**, or email the
   conference contact with the graphic attached.

## Immediate: Nicaragua post (just posted) → Surfers Medical Association

- **Find & tag:** search "Surfers Medical Association" on IG (likely @surfersmed or similar) and
  tag them in the post + first comment.
- **DM to send today:**

> Hi! We run convene.md — a free interactive map of medical & surgical conferences worldwide.
> We featured your upcoming Nicaragua conference in a post today because it's exactly the kind of
> "use your CME to see the world" meeting our audience loves. Tag/link is all yours — feel free to
> reshare to your story or grid if it's useful to your members. Happy to send the original file.
> Keep up the great work getting clinicians in the water. 🤙

## Reusable DM template (swap the italics)

> Hi! We run convene.md, a free interactive map of medical & surgical conferences worldwide. We
> featured *YOUR CONFERENCE* in a post today — our audience is clinicians planning CME travel, so
> it's a natural fit. Feel free to reshare to your story/grid; happy to send the original graphic.
> And if any of your other meetings aren't on the map yet, send them our way and we'll add them.

*(The last line doubles as a data-sourcing ask — societies know their own calendars better than anyone.)*

## Beyond societies — the daily 15-minute engagement loop
(from marketing/instagram.md) — comment substantively on 5 posts from target accounts, reply to
every comment on ours, follow 10–15 relevant accounts. Manual only; automated following gets
accounts flagged.

## Also, when convenient (compounding channels, from the growth plan)
- **Reddit** r/InternetIsBeautiful (interactive map sites do well there) + r/medicine self-promo thread
- **Physician Facebook groups** (Physician Side Gigs, physician-finance/travel) — "I built a free map of every surgical conference"
