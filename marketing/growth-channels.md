# convene.md — beyond Instagram: where growth actually comes from

**Reality check:** a <1-month-old IG account with static image posts reaches almost no
non-followers. Instagram's discovery engine is **Reels**, and its growth engine for a new
account is **borrowed reach** (someone established resharing you). And our audience — clinicians
with CME budgets — mostly congregates OFF Instagram. Optimize for *qualified link clicks*, not
follower count.

Three unlocks, highest leverage first.

---

## 1. Borrowed reach — get featured by accounts that already have the audience

New accounts grow by being amplified, not by posting into the void.

- **Society reshares** (see marketing/outreach.md) — tag + DM every society we feature. One
  reshare from a society = hundreds of perfect-fit eyeballs.
- **Collab posts** — IG's "Collab" feature puts one post on TWO accounts' grids (yours + a
  partner's), shown to both audiences. Ask a CME provider / a physician-creator / a society to
  co-author a destination post.
- **Repost accounts** — medical meme/education pages (@medicine.memes-type, @figs, residency
  pages) repost useful/funny content. DM them the map with a hook: "free tool your followers
  will actually use."
- **Feature swaps** — find 3–5 small physician-travel / med-education accounts of similar size;
  agree to each share the other's best post to stories. Mutual, free, warm.

---

## 2. Reels — Instagram's only real discovery surface

Static posts reach followers. Reels reach strangers (Reels feed + Explore). For a new account,
Reels are ~10× the organic reach. The good news: our product screen-records into a *great* Reel —
a world map filling with colorful pins is inherently satisfying.

**How to film (no equipment):** screen-record convene.md on your phone (or a screen capture of
the desktop), 15–25s, add on-screen text + a trending/upbeat audio. Post as a Reel.

### Reel scripts (ready to film)

**Reel A — "the flex"**
- Hook text (0–2s): "POV: your hospital gives you a CME budget 💳"
- Screen record: open convene.md → filter to your specialty → zoom into Hawaii, then Switzerland, then a surf break
- Mid text: "and you can spend it… anywhere there's a conference"
- End card: "160+ surgical meetings, one map — convene.md"
- Caption: short, + link-in-bio nudge. Audio: upbeat/trending.

**Reel B — "5-second search"**
- Hook: "Every conference in your specialty. 5 seconds. Watch."
- Screen record: open site → tap Specialty → pick "Orthopedic Surgery" → List view → scroll the dates
- End: "Free. No login. convene.md"
- This one sells the *utility* — best for saves/shares among residents.

**Reel C — "conferences in places you'd actually go"**
- Hook: "3 surgical conferences worth the flight ✈️"
- Quick cuts (2–3s each) of pins + popups: Snowmass (ski + spine), Nicaragua (surf medicine),
  Cairo (pyramids + peds neuro)
- End: "convene.md — where's your next one?"

**Reel D — "ski + scrub"** (save for late fall/winter)
- Hook: "Earn CME on a chairlift 🎿"
- AO Davos / Mayfield Snowmass angle + the ski post graphic
- End: "The ski-CME meetings, mapped — convene.md"

*Repurpose:* every static post we've made can become a Reel by screen-recording the map filtered
to that conference's region while the caption plays as on-screen text.

## 3. Go where physicians already are (higher ROI than IG for this audience)

### LinkedIn — probably the single best channel (allows links, professional audience)
Post as **yourself** (founder voice out-performs a brand page 10×). Ready-to-post:

> A year ago I wasted an afternoon hunting for surgical conference dates across a dozen society
> websites, PDFs, and dead links. So I built the thing I wanted: an interactive world map of
> every surgical conference — filter by specialty and year, see it on a map, add it to your
> calendar in one click.
>
> It started as a surgery project (I'm a surgeon). It's now 160+ meetings across 6 continents,
> and growing every week.
>
> The idea I keep coming back to: your CME budget is basically a travel grant with a dress code.
> Use it. There's a conference in Cape Town, in Cairo, on a Swiss mountain, at a Baja surf break.
>
> It's free, no login: convene.md — would love feedback from anyone who plans CME travel.

*Post it, then reply to every comment. Re-share to relevant LinkedIn groups (surgery, GME,
medical education). This one post likely out-reaches a month of IG.*

### Physician Facebook groups (huge, warm, link-friendly)
Target groups: **Physician Side Gigs**, **Physicians on FIRE / physician-finance**, specialty
resident/attending groups, **Doctors on Instagram/social** communities. Post (adapt per group's
rules — many require it be genuinely useful, not spammy):

> Made a free tool I wish existed in residency: a map of every surgical conference worldwide,
> filterable by specialty + year, with one-click calendar export. No login, no ads. Sharing in
> case it helps anyone planning where to spend their CME allowance this year — convene.md.
> (Happy to add any meeting you don't see on it.)

### Reddit
- **r/InternetIsBeautiful** — interactive map sites do genuinely well here; could spike traffic.
  Title: "I mapped every surgical conference in the world (filter by specialty, add to calendar)".
- **r/medicine**, **r/surgery**, specialty subs — post in their self-promo / weekly threads only,
  framed as a free resource + "tell me what's missing."

### Doximity / KevinMD
- Doximity: share in relevant groups; it's the physician social network.
- KevinMD or Op-Med (Doximity) guest piece: "Use your CME budget to see the world" with the tool
  as the practical takeaway. Evergreen referral traffic + a backlink (helps SEO + domain age).

## The mindset shift
- **Followers ≠ goal.** A LinkedIn post seen by 5,000 physicians beats 200 IG followers.
- **Utility > aesthetics** for *this* audience — the "find your conference in 5 seconds" angle
  converts harder than pretty travel photos (though those build brand).
- **Every channel adds an inbound link** → ages the domain's reputation → chips away at the
  corporate-filter/NRD blocking too. Double win.
- **Give it 4–8 weeks.** One society reshare or one Reel that pops can 10× a small account
  overnight; the trend matters, not any single day.
